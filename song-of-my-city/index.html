Movie Night: Song of My City

Goodside gathers to celebrate our very own John Sears, whose work as editor on this short film earned a spot at Telluride.

Song of My City, directed by David C. Roberts, is a cinematic montage that reconstructs 1970s New York as it exists in the movies that mythologized it—Taxi Driver, The Warriors, The Taking of Pelham 123. Seventeen minutes of neon, steam, and a skyline that no longer exists.
