The Dark and Stormy Party

The Dark and Stormy was invented more than 100 years ago when members of Bermuda’s Royal Naval Officer’s Club added a splash of local rum to their spicy homemade ginger beer. They described its ominous hue as “the color of a cloud only a fool or dead man would sail under.”

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz. Gosling's Black Seal dark rum
  • 3 oz. ginger beer
  • 1/2 oz. lime juice

Directions:

  1. Fill a Collins glass with ice.
  2. Pour dark rum over the ice.
  3. Add a layer of lime juice, then top with ginger beer.
  4. Garnish with a lime wedge.
