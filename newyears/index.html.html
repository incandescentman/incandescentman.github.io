<div id=“vidtop-content”> <div class=“vid-info”> <h1>Cocktails on Saint Marks</h1> <p></p> </ol> <p> Please join me this Sunday in order that we might gather together as a family one last time before the great crossing over. Take note, this party is but a prologue, a waystation to fortify you on your journey before I send you out into the night. We will convene here on Saint Marks Place at the unfashionably early hour of 5:00 o&rsquo;clock to carouse and commune before adjourning promptly at 9:00pm and scattering across the boroughs. </p> <p> The house will offer a limited menu of cocktails, and Eli Shallcross, our resident pro bono mixologist, will be serving his notorious Touch of Evil Punch, his signature blend of gin, mezcal, raspberry, and lemons. Your own civilian contributions of wine and bottled spirits are also welcome. Feel free to bring friends. </p>
