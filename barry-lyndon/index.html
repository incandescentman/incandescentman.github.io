Barry Lyndon Screening

Let us gather for an intimate screening of Stanley Kubrick's masterwork on 18th century ambition and artful cinematography. I’ll transform the living room into a miniature cinema with velvet drapes, candlelight, and a projector calibrated for every luminous frame.

Before the film rolls, we’ll sip sherry and nibble on tea sandwiches; during the intermission we can debate whether duels or powdered wigs are the true currency of the aristocracy. Feel free to bring a favorite beverage or snack that nods to the period.

The film is long, sumptuous, and best enjoyed with friends who appreciate slow cinema, so arrive on time and settle in for the full experience.
